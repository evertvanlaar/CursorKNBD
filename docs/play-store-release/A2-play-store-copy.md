### Voorstel — volledige tekst (copy-paste ready)

```
Kala Nera Guide is your installable local companion for Kala Nera and Pelion, Greece. Discover where to eat, drink, stay, shop, and find services—plus plan trips with bus timetables and practical travel information.

WHY KALA NERA GUIDE?
• Made for visitors and locals in Kala Nera, Kato Gatzea, Koropi and the wider Pelion coast
• Fast search, categories, and A–Z browsing
• Works on the go as a lightweight app (PWA) from the Play Store

EXPLORE LOCAL BUSINESSES
• Curated directory: eat, drink, sleep, shop, camp, rent, travel & more
• Filter by area (Kala Nera, Kato Gatzea, Koropi)
• Open business pages with contact details, maps, and website links
• Save places to Favorites on your device (no account required)

BUS TIMETABLE (KALA NERA)
• Check departures from the Kala Nera stop
• Switch direction, day, and time-of-day filters
• Helpful for trips toward Volos and villages across Pelion

PELION GUIDE — PRACTICAL INFO
• Flights: seasonal routes at Nea Anchialos (VOL)
• Regional events and village traditions
• Walking inspiration (external English guide)
• Useful local and emergency phone numbers

OFFLINE & PRIVACY
• Caches content for faster repeat visits
• Limited offline access to previously viewed data
• Connected to kalanera.gr; privacy policy on the website
• Uses analytics and light operational telemetry; favorites stored locally on your device

QUESTIONS OR FEEDBACK?
Email info@spiti.tech

Video trailer: credits to Dejan MP.
```

**Geschatte lengte:** ~1.350 characters (ruim onder 4000 — ruimte voor EL-sectie of toekomstige features).
